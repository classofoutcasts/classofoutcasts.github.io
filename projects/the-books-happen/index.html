Metaflesh
2015

Artist book (prosthetic silicone, silicone paint, prints, plastic sheets). The Books Happen, Kunstruimte Kuub, Utrecht, NL.

The result of the collaborative artist project The Books Happen iniated by visual artist Henny Overbeek. The project spans over three years and 50 unique productions of collaborative artists books. Offering collages dummies to other artists with a carte blanche. The dynamics of these collaborations are manifested on the pages of collage, archival footgage, drawing and sometimes violent action or complete disappearance.
